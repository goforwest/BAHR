**Primary revenue driver for individual users**

### Product Tiers

#### Free Tier: "Explorer"
- **Pricing:** $0
- **Limits:** 10 verses/month, basic explanations
- **Purpose:** User acquisition, viral growth
- **Target:** 2,000 free users by Month 12

#### Researcher Tier: "Scholar"
- **Pricing:** $19/month or $190/year
- **Features:** Unlimited analysis, batch upload (100 verses), API access (10k calls/month)
- **Target:** PhD students, individual researchers, serious poets
- **Conversion:** 3% of free users

#### Professional Tier: "Research Team"
- **Pricing:** $49/month or $490/year
- **Features:** Everything + team collaboration (5 users), white-label reports, 50k API calls/month
- **Target:** Research teams, publishers, literary organizations
- **Conversion:** 1% of free users, 20% upgrade from Researcher

### Revenue Model Economics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
- **Organic:** ~$20/user (content marketing, SEO, conferences)
- **Paid:** ~$50/user (if running ads later)
- **Target:** Primarily organic to keep CAC low

**Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- **Researcher:** $228/year √ó 2 years avg = $456 LTV
- **Professional:** $588/year √ó 3 years avg = $1,764 LTV
- **LTV:CAC ratio:** 22:1 organic, 9:1 paid (excellent)

**Churn Rate:**
- **Target:** <5% monthly (60% annual retention)
- **Academic users:** Lower churn (tied to research projects)
- **Poets:** Higher churn (seasonal use)

### Conversion Funnel Optimization

**Free User Journey:**
1. **Discovery:** Academic network, conference presentation, HackerNews
2. **Activation:** Sign up, analyze first verse (80% within 7 days goal)
3. **Engagement:** Analyze 3+ verses (50% of activated users)
4. **Hit limit:** Use 10th verse (30% of engaged users)
5. **Convert:** Upgrade to Researcher (10% of users who hit limit = 3% overall)

**Conversion Tactics:**
- **In-app prompts:** "Verse 7/10 analyzed this month. Upgrade for unlimited?"
- **Email triggers:** Day 7 (no activity), Day 14 (50% limit), Day 21 (hit limit)
- **Social proof:** "Join 500+ researchers using BAHR" (update monthly)
- **Time pressure:** "Limited time: Get 20% off first 3 months"
